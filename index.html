intro

Welcome to a look into my world. I am a graphic designer with specialities in illustration and typography. This website will provide some examples of my work and motivations behind them. To check out my full portfolio click here!

Throughout my life I have always loved to create things, no matter the medium. When I arrived at college, that was no exception. I spent my first semester as a Spanish major, and quickly realized that art could not be that far removed from my life. So, by my second semester, I had switched to Graphic Design and began to discover all the magic things you can do with a screen, keyboard, and mouse. As of August 2023, I landed my first graphic design job at a spiritwear company, and I could not be happier to continue expanding my graphic design knowledge and well as my print knowledge.

works

artist statement

A sense of wonder is always necessary in my artwork. My art practice has always revolved around this idea, no matter the medium or inspiration. Thinking intuitively, without a hard set plan for my artwork, I allow the materials and process to guide me in the direction the work is headed towards. Graphic design, photography, and ceramics are all media I use for my pieces because they allow my aesthetic to travel across different dimensions and bring different perspectives to my art. This trip through dimensions correlates to the inspiration in my work that is derived from counterculture from the ‘60s and ‘70s. Nick Sand, one of the men who synthesized LSD, said that he wanted to “turn on the world” and bring a sense of wonder and enlightenment to those who took his LSD. In my artwork, a sense of wonder and a whimsical nature are the most prominent feelings, and this quote reminds me of these feelings I want to portray.

animation

WIRED, 2022. This animation piece depicts actions that can be associated with the word "wired." After my sophomore year, I was diagnosed with ADHD. Although I was glad to more fully understand my brain and how it functions best, getting diagnosed late in life only furthered feelings of being lost and unprepared for "the real world." Wired is how I feel in many aspects of my life. Wired off adderall and coffee, shaky, but laser focused. Wired off sex, hyperaware and an easy kind of focused. Wired off drugs like alcohol, buzzing with energy and focused on all the wrong things. You get two sides of the same coin with this feeling, it is your own choice which side of the coin you stay on. < WIRED
